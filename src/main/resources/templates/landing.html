// ABOUT

A puzzle game about a sliding horse

Your horse Konek Tobey slides in one direction until he hits a wall — no stopping halfway. Every move is a commitment. Plan ahead, collect all the flowers, and clear the maze.

12 handcrafted levels ranging from casual warm-ups to hard puzzles. Compete on the global leaderboard and chase your personal best time and score.

🐴

Sliding mechanic

Tobey slides until he hits a wall. Think ahead — every move counts.

🌸

Collect flowers

Pick up all flowers before reaching the goal to clear the level.

🏆

Score for speed

Fewer steps and faster time means a higher score. Beat the leaderboard.
